Jeju Island, South Korea

Jeju Island, South Korea

Jeju Island, South Korea's largest island, is a volcanic wonderland known for its scenic beaches, lush landscapes, and unique cultural heritage. Home to the Hallasan Mountain, a dormant volcano, and the iconic lava tubes, it offers a blend of natural beauty and adventure, making it a popular destination for travelers.
