Dream Zen Abodes

Explore our dream locations around the world where we envision creating harmonious Zen Abodes. Each location has been carefully selected for its unique cultural significance, natural beauty, and potential to foster community and spiritual growth.

Auroville, India

Auroville is an experimental township in Viluppuram district, mostly in the state of Tamil Nadu, India, with some parts in the Union Territory of Pondicherry. It was founded in 1968 by Mirra Alfassa and designed by architect Roger Anger.

Northern Light - Akureyri, Iceland

Akureyri, Iceland's charming northern gem, nestles by Eyjafjörður, boasting majestic mountain backdrops. Known as the "Capital of the North," it offers vibrant culture and serene Botanical Garden.

Taj Mahal, India

The Taj Mahal, India's architectural masterpiece in Agra, is a UNESCO World Heritage site. Built by Emperor Shah Jahan in the 17th century in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal.

Colosseum, Rome

The Colosseum in Rome, Italy, an ancient amphitheater built in 70-80 AD, is an iconic symbol of Roman engineering and entertainment. Hosting gladiator contests and spectacles for thousands.

Victoria Falls, Zambia

Victoria Falls, located on the Zambezi River, defines the border between Zambia and Zimbabwe. Known as "The Smoke That Thunders," it's one of the world's largest waterfalls.

Paracutin Volcano, Mexico

Parícutin Volcano in Mexico emerged suddenly in 1943 from a farmer's field, growing to over 1,300 feet. Its eruption was witnessed, and its creation studied, making it a unique landmark.
