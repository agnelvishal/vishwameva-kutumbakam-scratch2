Frequently Asked Questions

What is Vishwameva Kutumbakam?

Vishwameva Kutumbakam means "The World is One Family". We are a community dedicated to creating Zen Abodes where people from all walks of life can live in harmony with nature and each other.

How can I join the community?

You can join by exploring our Membership Types. We offer Founder, Citizen, and Denizen memberships, each with different levels of engagement and benefits.

Where are the Zen Abodes located?

We have actualized abodes in Auroville, Nepal, and Bali, with many more dream locations initiated around the globe.

Can I visit without being a member?

Yes, many of our Zen Abodes are open to visitors. However, members enjoy priority booking and exclusive access to certain events and areas.

What does "Free Range" mean?

In our context, it means living freely and authentically, disconnected from the stressors of modern urban life and reconnected with your true self.
